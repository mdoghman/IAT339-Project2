Programs

At Al-Hidaya Elementary we offer a large variety of programs to provide your children with a flexible, but thorough educational experience. We emphasize a cohorted learning system where students will learn together with the same peers for all 8 years of their elementary education. This allows our students to build stronger relationships as well as teamwork abilities in order to become leaders in their communities.
