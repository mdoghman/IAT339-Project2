Welcome to Al-Hidayah Elementary

.

Al-Hidaya Elementary has been designed to offer the latest in Arabic language tutelage as well as to exceed the learning outcomes provided for the K-7 grade range as perscribed by the British Columbia Provincial Government. Al-Hidayah was established to satisfy urgent local community needs. The Islamic Center in New Westminster opened Al-Hidayah School with the goal of providing the children of our community with an excellent secular education and superior Islamic instruction. This is facilitated through a strong curriculum based on the standards set by the State of California, the Islamic teachings taught by qualified teachers, and Arabic language courses taught by native Arabic speakers. Feel free to explore our website, our history and to learn more about the culture that we are trying to perserve within future generations!
