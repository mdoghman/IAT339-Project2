Kindergarten

The Al-Hidaya Kindergarten program is open to all children ages 4-5 who have not already completed an equivalent level of study.

All students will recieve daily Arabic instruction in addition to other fundamental learning principles perscribed by the BC Provincial government. A further breakdown of our learning outcomes can be found on the Education BC Website.

Pricing

The total amount for the kindergarten registration (including all school trips) is $800.
